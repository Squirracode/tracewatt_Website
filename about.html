Our Working Process

How We Work Process In Solar Energy

01

Real-Time Data Monitoring with AI Insights

Every 5 minutes, Tracewatt collects live data from inverters, meters, and sensors. AI-driven analytics process this data instantly, providing deep insights into string-wise generation, detecting inefficiencies, and predicting potential failures.

02

AI-Powered Fault Detection & Prediction

Advanced AI algorithms analyze historical and real-time data to identify anomalies, predict faults before they occur, and send automated alerts—ensuring proactive maintenance and peak performance.

03

Remote AI-Enhanced Control & Automation

Manage and optimize your solar plant from anywhere with AI-assisted automation. Tracewatt’s intelligent system suggests corrective actions, enables remote troubleshooting, and streamlines operations with role-based access.

04

Smart Reporting & AI-Driven Optimization

AI-generated reports provide advanced energy yield analysis, efficiency trends, and actionable recommendations, helping users maximize output and ensure long-term solar system health.
