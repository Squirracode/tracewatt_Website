Privacy Policy

At Tracewatt, a product proudly developed by Squirracode Technologies, your privacy is more than a priority—it’s a promise. Whether you're exploring our website tracewatt.com or utilizing the Tracewatt RMS (Remote Monitoring System) portal, we are dedicated to ensuring your personal data is protected with care, integrity, and the highest industry standards.

We collect personal information only when it is essential to enhance your experience or provide services tailored to your needs. All data collection is done transparently—with your consent, your knowledge, and a clear explanation of how the information will be used.

Your trust is our most valuable asset. That’s why we retain information only as long as necessary and protect it using robust, industry-approved security protocols to guard against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Squirracode Technologies does not sell or share your personally identifiable information with third parties, except when required by law. Your data is yours, and we treat it with the discretion and confidentiality it deserves.

While our platforms may link to external sites for your convenience, we do not govern those websites and cannot take responsibility for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies independently before interacting with them.

You always have the right to withhold personal information. While this may affect the range of services we can offer, we respect your decisions unconditionally and will continue to provide as much value as possible.

By using the Tracewatt website or the Tracewatt RMS portal, you acknowledge and accept our privacy practices as outlined here. If you have any questions or would like further clarity about how we handle your personal information, please do not hesitate to reach out—we’re here to help.

This policy is effective as of 1 January 2025.
